PALAWAN ISLAND


Palawan is a tropical paradise in the Philippines, celebrated for its breathtaking natural beauty and pristine Palawan,
the jewel of the Philippines, is an island province that unfolds a tropical paradise like no other. Stretching between the South
China Sea and the Sulu Sea, Palawan is a sanctuary of natural beauty and biodiversity. With its emerald jungles, soaring limestone
cliffs, and pristine beaches, it's often referred to as the "Last Frontier" of the Philippines.Puerto Princesa, the capital city, is
home to the awe-inspiring Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where a subterranean river navigates through
captivating limestone caves adorned with otherworldly formations.El Nido, in the northern part of Palawan, is renowned for its dramatic
karst landscapes, secluded lagoons, and hidden beaches. Coron, further to the north, is famed for its WWII shipwrecks that have become
incredible dive sites.Palawan's terrestrial and marine ecosystems are incredibly diverse, making it a biodiversity hotspot.
